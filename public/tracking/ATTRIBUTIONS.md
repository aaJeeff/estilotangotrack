# Tracking visual assets

- **Physical globe:** “Globo-Terráqueo-Mapa-Físico” by felipezaratemontero, sourced from Sketchfab and licensed under Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY). The model is loaded and rescaled for the tracking scene.
- **Obelisk:** created by Thingiverse user rubenv2006, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution.
- **Monumento a la Bandera:** created by Thingiverse user nachocg, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution–NonCommercial.
- **Earth textures:** project-provided atmospheric, normal and specular textures from `concept/assets/earth/`.

The original source notes and license files remain in `concept/assets/models/` and `models3d/`.
